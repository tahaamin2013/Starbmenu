Love sweet drinks? Say hello to Starbucks White Hot Chocolate! It's a yummy treat that'll warm you up on chilly days.

## What's in it?
Picture this: creamy white chocolate mixed with hot milk. On top? A fluffy cloud of whipped cream. Sounds good, right? It's like a hug in a cup!

## Who's it for?
Everyone! If you have a sweet tooth but don't like coffee, this drink is for you. Kids love it too!

## When to drink it?
Anytime! But it's extra nice when it's cold outside. It's the perfect way to warm up after a long day.

## Make it your way
Want to change things up? You can! Try:
- Different milk (like soy or almond)
- Less sugar
- Add fun flavors (how about vanilla?)
- No whipped cream

## Watch out!
This tasty drink is pretty rich. One cup has:
- 400 calories
- 48g sugar
- 18g fat
It's okay for a treat, but maybe not every day.
